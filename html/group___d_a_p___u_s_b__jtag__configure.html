DAP_JTAG_Configure
JTAG Commands

Configure JTAG Chain.

The DAP_JTAG_Configure Command sets the JTAG chain information.

DAP_JTAG_Configure Command:

BYTE | BYTE *| BYTE *****|
> 0x15 | Count | IR Length |
******|*******|+++++++++++|

DAP_JTAG_Configure Response:

BYTE | BYTE **|
< 0x15 | Status |
******|********|
  • Count: Number of devices in chain
  • IR Length: JTAG IR Length (in bits) for each device
  • Status: Response Status
